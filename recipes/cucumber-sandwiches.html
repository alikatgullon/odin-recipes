Cucumber Sandwiches

Description

These fresh, creamy cucumber sandwiches are my favorite to have at any teaparty or picnique. Just cut, trim, whip, and serve! I've made these in the past for my cross country team and they were a blast :D. The original recipe is by Holly at Spend With Pennies.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese softened
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill chopped
  • 1 tsp fresh chives chopped
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • salt & pepperto taste
  • 1 long English cucumber thinly sliced
  • 1 loaf sliced bread crusts removed

Instructions

  1. With a hand mixer, mix cream cheese and mayonnaise until smooth. Stir in herbs, garlic powder, and salt & pepper to taste
  2. Spread the cream cheese mixture onto one side of each piece of bread.
  3. Thinly slice cucumbers. Layer over half of the bread slices. Top with additional herbs if desired.
  4. Top with remaining bread slice, remove crusts if desired and cut each sandwich into three pieces.
  5. Serve immediately or cover and store up to 24 hours.

Notes

Leftover sandwiches can be stored covered in the fridge for up to 24 hours.
